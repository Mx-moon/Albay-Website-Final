Welcome to Albay

Nestled in the heart of the Bicol Peninsula, Albay is a province that captivates every visitor with its extraordinary natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. At the center of it all stands Mayon Volcano — the world's most perfect volcanic cone.

Beyond Mayon, Albay offers pristine beaches, ancient ruins, lush hills, vibrant festivals, and unforgettable cuisine. Whether you are an adventure seeker, a history enthusiast, a foodie, or someone who simply wants to relax — Albay has something special waiting for you.
